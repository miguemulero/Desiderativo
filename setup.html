Setup Instructions for NotebookLM Bridge Bookmarklet

Follow the steps below to configure the NotebookLM bridge bookmarklet:

  1. Open your web browser.
  2. Create a new bookmark:
    • In most browsers, you can do this by right-clicking the bookmarks bar and selecting "Add Page" or "Add Bookmark".
  3. Name your bookmark, e.g., "NotebookLM Bridge".
  4. Copy and paste the following code into the URL field of the bookmark: 
                javascript:(function(){alert('Hello from NotebookLM!');})();
  5. Save the bookmark.
  6. To use the bookmarklet, simply click on the bookmark whenever you want to activate NotebookLM functionality on a page.

Enjoy using your NotebookLM bridge bookmarklet!
